<div class="lang-content lang-en" markdown="1">

I am an M.S. student in Information Engineering at **Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU)**.

My recent work focuses on **LLM agents**, **multi-agent reinforcement learning**, **reasoning**, and **alignment**. I am particularly interested in building practical agent systems for real-world decision-making and dialogue tasks.

## Research Interests

- LLM-based agent systems and multi-agent workflows
- RLHF / preference optimization / reward modeling
- Long-context code understanding and compression
- Task-oriented dialogue and social intelligence in LLMs

## Experience Highlights

- **ByteDance Seed (Algorithm Intern, 2025.09 - 2025.12):** participated in architecture design and optimization for Doubao multi-agent systems.
- **Taotian Group, Xianyu (Algorithm Intern, 2025.06 - 2025.09):** worked on multi-agent and E2E upgrades for AI consignment; explored MARL methods for social dialogue and bargaining tasks.
- **ByteDance Trae (Algorithm Intern, 2025.01 - 2025.05):** designed multi-agent workflow components and long-context code compression pipelines; improved recall and code merge efficiency.
- **Ant Group (LLM Algorithm Intern, 2024.06 - 2025.01):** built and evaluated multi-agent negotiation systems and reward models for debt collection dialogue.
